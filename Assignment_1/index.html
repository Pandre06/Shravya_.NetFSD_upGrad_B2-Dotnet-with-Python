Welcome to Guru Nanak Institute of Technology - GNIT

Guru Nanak Institute of Technology (GNIT), a premier institute in the field of engineering and management studies was established in 2008 by Barah Moula Educational Society and is located at Khanapur Village, Manchal Mandal, Ibrahimpatnam, Telangana State. The institute was earlier functioning under the name of JSN Institute of Engineering and Technology at Adilabad, Andhra Pradesh, since 1999, and later shifted to Ibrahimpatnam and renamed as Guru Nanak Institute of Technology after due the approval of AICTE and Govt. of Andhra Pradesh. Since its inception GNIT has been consistently being listed in the top engineering colleges in the state.
